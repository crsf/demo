How to apply for the OxCan Scholarship

Note: Applicants should approach the university of their choice before the selection process to ensure they have the required prerequisites for entry

Submit a written application, addressed to 'Canada Scholarship', at registrar@rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk. Your application must include:

  • Your resumé
  • Photocopy of your university academic transcript, complete or in progress (a copy of your secondary school performance would also be helpful)
  • Evidence of age and UK Citizenship (birth certificate or passport in colour photocopy)
  • A personal statement
    • What you should include
  • Four references sent by your referees
    • What you need to do

Applications must be received prior to ? November 2017

Questions on process can be addressed to 'Canada Scholarship', registrar@rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk

Selection criteria

Canada scholars are selected based on:

  • academic excellence
  • character
  • leadership potential, as demonstrated through extra-curricular involvement

Selection of Canada Scholars is made without regard to gender, gender identity, marital status, sexual orientation, race, ethnic origin, colour, religion, social background, caste, or disability.
