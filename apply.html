How to apply for the Oxford-Canada Scholarship

Submit a written application by email to: ox-can.scholarship@rhodeshouse.ox.ac.uk. Your application must include:

  • Scanned copy of your university academic transcript, complete or in progress (a copy of your secondary school performance would also be helpful)

  • A personal statement
    • What you should include
  • Four references sent by your referees
    • What you need to do

Applications must be received prior to closing on 16 November 2017

Questions on process can be addressed by email to: enquiries@Oxford-Canada.org

Note: Applicants should approach the university of their choice before the selection process to ensure they have the required prerequisites for entry

Selection criteria

Oxford-Canada scholars are selected based on:

  • academic excellence
  • character
  • leadership potential, as demonstrated through extra-curricular involvement

Selection of Oxford-Canada Scholars is made without regard to gender, gender identity, marital status, sexual orientation, race, ethnic origin, colour, religion, social background, caste, or disability.
