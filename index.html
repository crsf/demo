The Oxford to Canada Scholarship

Funded by the Canadian Rhodes Scholars Foundation (CRSF)

Who can apply

  • Education: Must be studying at or have graduated from Oxford within the last 24 months. Both undergraduate and graduate students can apply
  • Age: Must be under 26 years of age on December 31 of the year applying
  • Citizenship: Must be a UK citizen
