The Oxford to Canada Scholarship

Funded by the Canadian Rhodes Scholars Foundation (CRSF)

What the scholarship covers

  • Tuition and student fees for full-time, postgraduate study at any Canadian university, renewable for a second year
  • The Scholarship is open to all disciplines; there are no restrictions on the course of study.
  • Stipend of CA$ 16,000 per year
  • 1 travel grant of CA$ 1,000 for travel to Canada
  • 1 travel grant of CA$ 1,000 for travel within Canada, while on stipend
  • Practical advice from CRSF Board Members on preparation for study and issues arising while in Canada

Who can apply

  • Education: Must be studying at or have graduated from Oxford within the last 24 months. Both undergraduate and graduate students can apply
  • Age: Must be under 26 years of age on December 31 of the year applying
  • Citizenship: Must be a UK citizen
