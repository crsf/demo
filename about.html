About the Canadian Rhodes Scholars Foundation

CRSF was founded by Canadian Rhodes Scholars including Roland Michener, a Governor General of Canada (Alberta, Hertford College, 1919). For the historical notes written by a founding director, Michael Howarth (Ontario, Queen's, 1949), please see the historical notes page.

The Foundation possesses a modest endownment accumulated through legacies made by Ralph Henson (Nova Scotia, Exeter College, 1928) and other Rhodes Scholars.

The Foundation is governed by a board of directors. To learn more, see the board members.

The Foundation is closely tied to the membership of the Canadian Association of Rhodes Scholars but is a distinct entity.
