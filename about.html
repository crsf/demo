About the Canadian Rhodes Scholars Foundation

CRSF was founded by Canadian Rhodes Scholars including Roland Michener, a Governor General of Canada (Alberta, Hertford College, 1919). To learn more, see the history of the Canadian Rhodes Scholars Foundation written by another of the founding directors, Michael Howarth (Ontario, Queen's, 1949).

  • History of the Canadian Rhodes Scholars Foundation

The Foundation possesses a modest endownment accumulated through legacies made by Ralph Henson (Nova Scotia, Exeter College, 1928) and other Rhodes Scholars.

The Foundation is governed by a board of directors.

  • Current board members

The Foundation is closely tied to the membership of the Canadian Association of Rhodes Scholars but is a distinct entity.
