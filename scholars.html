Rebecca Pearse

Queen's College, Oxford, 2017

  • Research focus: Masters in Neuroscience
  • University: McGill University

Ms Pearse graduated from Oxford with a contratulatory first in Experimental Psychology. She will study for a Masters in Neuroscience, at McGill University. There, she says, she looks forward to working with world-class supervisors, in the areas of language cognition and addiction therapy, making use of McGill's large range and high quality laboratories. She adds that she looks forward to living in the cosmopolitan, bilingual and culturally exciting city of Montreal. After her Masters, she plans to work toward a PhD in Neuroscience, in Canada or elsewhere.
