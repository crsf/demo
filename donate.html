The Canadian Rhodes Scholars Foundation (CRSF) is funded by Canadian Rhodes Scholars, former Oxford-Canada Scholars and other generous contributors, interested in furthering the Foundation's work. The CRSF is most grateful for recent, generous gifts from the McCall MacBain Foundation and the Ches Crosbie Success Foundation.

The Foundation awards the Oxford-Canada Scholarship, which offers graduates of Oxford University an opportunity to further their studies at a Canadian university of their choice. One or two Oxford-Canada Scholarships are awarded, annually. They fund full-time, postgraduate study, renewable for a second year, and are open to all disciplines; there are no restrictions on the course of study.
