What you should include in the personal statement

Part 1, should list:

  • the field in which you wish to study (the more specific the better) and why Canada is preferred for such study
  • universities under consideration
  • duration of the course

Part 2, the chance to tell your story, including:

  • why you chose this academic field
  • your intended career path and planned contribution to the greater good
  • details of accomplishments, extra-curricular activities, community service
